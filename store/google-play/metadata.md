## Full Description

Power Cabinet is a political fantasy-card game where you draft a cabinet of world leaders, score promise receipts, and survive the truth tax.

Build a five-card squad, choose a captain, follow market-style signals, and climb the league table. Power Cabinet is designed as an entertainment game around public political narratives. It does not include real-money betting, trading, financial advice, or in-app market execution.
