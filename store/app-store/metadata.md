# App Store Metadata

## Name

Power Cabinet

## Subtitle

Draft leaders. Score promises.

## Promotional Text

Build a fantasy cabinet of world leaders, track promise receipts, and turn political chaos into collectible squad strategy.

## Description

Power Cabinet is a political fantasy-card game where you draft a five-card cabinet, react to promise receipts, and climb the league table through market heat, momentum, and the truth tax.

Swipe through leader cards, build a squad, choose a captain, and compare your score against rival leagues. The app is for entertainment and civic media literacy. It does not offer betting, trading, financial advice, or real-money market execution.

## Keywords

## Age Rating Notes

Political/news themes. No gambling, no real-money trading, no user-generated chat.

## Privacy Notes

No account is required for the local demo flow. No personal data is needed for core gameplay.
